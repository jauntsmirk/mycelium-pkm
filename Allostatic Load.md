
Mental stress, everyday pressure, and physical load

## How to measure allostatic load to know when you need to rest?

Signs of allostatic load being too high
- you wake up tired even after a full night's sleep
- easily irritated
- workouts feel hard
- motivation drops
- aches & stiffness
- focus is off

Over training and testosterone
- Male athletes training more than 7 hours per week had a drop in testosterone of about 10%

## What not to do on a rest day
- Do not work extra
- Do not train hard
- Do not party all night

Sleep 8-10 hours
Eat a lot
drink a lot of water
chill
sauna, massage, foam-rolling

passive - when you're tired
active - when it lowers stress


## How to structure rest days

- no perfect rest day schedule
- adjust training based on 3 variables
	- how we're feeling
	- how fatigued we are
	- adapting to training?

measure total stress from 1-5 in responsibilities, work, training. whenever all goes up at once, take a rest.
